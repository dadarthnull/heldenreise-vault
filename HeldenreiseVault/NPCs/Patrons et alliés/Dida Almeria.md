## Kensei Blademaster

**Description:** A warrior of unparalleled discipline, clad in lacquered armor adorned with the symbols of a fallen lord. Every movement is deliberate, economical, a lifetime of training distilled into lethal efficiency. Their katana is an extension of their soulâ€”never drawn without purpose, never sheathed without blood. In their eyes burns the cold fire of absolute focus, a mind emptied of all but the next cut.

**Type:** Humanoid
**Difficulty:** 16
**Bonus:** +10
**Speed:** 35ft
**HP:** 160
**DPR:** 44
**Atk:** 3
**Dmg:** 15 per attack

---

**Focus Mechanic:** The Blademaster begins combat with 0 Focus. Maximum Focus: 5. Focus is gained through attacks and spent to activate techniques.

---

**Base - Focused Strike:** Melee weapon attack, reach 5ft, one target. Deals **15 [2d6+7]** slashing damage. The Blademaster gains **1 Focus**.

- *At 3+ Focus:* The attack deals an additional **7 [2d6]** slashing damage.
- *At 5 Focus:* The attack scores a critical hit on a roll of 18, 19, or 20.

---

**Signature - Perfect Cut (Recharge 5-6):** The Blademaster makes a single devastating strike against one creature within 10ft. The attack automatically hits and deals **35 [6d8+7]** slashing damage. The Blademaster gains **2 Focus**. If the Blademaster is at 5 Focus when using this ability, the target must make a *Constitution* saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn.

---

**Reaction - Way of the Blade:** Usable once per round. The Blademaster chooses one:

- *Steel Meets Steel:* When targeted by a melee attack, the Blademaster can make a contested attack roll. If the Blademaster wins, the attack misses and the Blademaster can immediately make one Base attack against the attacker.
- *Deflecting Cut:* When targeted by a ranged attack, the Blademaster adds **+5** to their AC against that attack. If this causes the attack to miss, the Blademaster gains **1 Focus**.
- *Punishing Counter:* When a creature within 5ft misses the Blademaster with an attack, the Blademaster makes one Base attack against that creature with advantage.

---

**Feat - Iron Resolve (1 Focus):** When the Blademaster takes damage, they can spend 1 Focus to reduce that damage by **20**.

**Feat - Inevitable Cut (2 Focus):** Before making a Base attack, the Blademaster can spend 2 Focus. The attack ignores cover, shield bonuses to AC, and cannot be avoided by reactions such as Shield or Uncanny Dodge.

**Feat - Blade Storm (3 Focus):** As an action, the Blademaster spends 3 Focus and makes a Base attack against every creature within 10ft.

**Feat - Death Before Dishonor (3 Focus):** When the Blademaster would be reduced to 0 HP, they can spend 3 Focus to remain at 1 HP instead. Until the end of their next turn, the Blademaster has advantage on all attacks and their Base attacks deal an additional **10 [3d6]** slashing damage.
