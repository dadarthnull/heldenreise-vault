## Abyssal Knight

**Description:** Encased in jagged black armor that seems to absorb light itself, this warrior has embraced the darkness that others flee. Veins of crimson pulse across their plate like bleeding wounds that never heal, and their greatsword drips with shadows that writhe hungrily toward the living. They do not fight despite their painâ€”they fight because of it. Every drop of blood spilled nearby, friend or foe, feeds the void within them. The more the battle costs, the more terrible they become.

**Type:** Humanoid
**Difficulty:** 16
**Bonus:** +9
**Speed:** 30ft
**HP:** 170
**DPR:** 42
**Atk:** 2
**Dmg:** 21 per attack

---

**Dark Points Mechanic:** The Abyssal Knight begins combat with 0 Dark Points. Maximum Dark Points: 5.

- Gain **1 Dark Point** when a creature within 30ft loses HP (maximum once per creature per round).
- Gain **2 Dark Points** when a creature within 30ft is reduced to 0 HP.
- Gain **1 Dark Point** when the Abyssal Knight loses 20 or more HP from a single source.

---

**Base - Abyssal Cleave:** Melee weapon attack, reach 10ft, one target. Deals **21 [3d10+5]** slashing damage plus **7 [2d6]** necrotic damage.

- *At 3+ Dark Points:* The attack deals an additional **7 [2d6]** necrotic damage.
- *At 5 Dark Points:* On hit, the target's maximum HP is reduced by **10** until they finish a long rest.

---

**Signature - Unleash Darkness (Recharge 5-6):** The Abyssal Knight releases the accumulated pain and death around them. All creatures within 20ft must make a *Constitution* saving throw. On failure, they take **35 [10d6]** necrotic damage and are frightened of the Abyssal Knight until the end of their next turn. On success, half damage and not frightened. The Abyssal Knight then gains HP equal to half the total damage dealt. This ability consumes all Dark Points and deals an additional **7 [2d6]** necrotic damage per Dark Point consumed.

---

**Reaction - Embrace the Void:** Usable once per round. The Abyssal Knight chooses one:

- *Feed on Pain:* When the Abyssal Knight takes damage, they can gain **2 Dark Points** and reduce the damage by **10**.
- *Shadow Counter:* When a creature within 10ft hits the Abyssal Knight with an attack, the Abyssal Knight can spend **1 Dark Point** to make one Base attack against that creature.
- *Death's Harvest:* When a creature within 30ft is reduced to 0 HP, the Abyssal Knight regains **20** HP and gains **1 Dark Point**.

---

**Feat - Bloodied Fury:** While at half HP or below, the Abyssal Knight's attacks automatically score critical hits. Additionally, the Abyssal Knight gains **1 Dark Point** at the start of each of their turns while at half HP or below.

**Feat - Soul Rend (2 Dark Points):** When the Abyssal Knight hits with a Base attack, they can spend 2 Dark Points. The target must make a *Wisdom* saving throw or take an additional **21 [6d6]** psychic damage and be unable to regain HP until the end of its next turn.

**Feat - Living Nightmare:** The Abyssal Knight is immune to the frightened condition. Additionally, creatures frightened of the Abyssal Knight take **7 [2d6]** psychic damage at the start of each of their turns.

**Feat - Dark Sacrifice:** As a bonus action, the Abyssal Knight can sacrifice their own vitality for power. They lose **20** HP (this damage cannot be reduced or prevented) and gain **3 Dark Points**. If this would reduce the Abyssal Knight to 0 HP, they instead drop to 1 HP and gain **5 Dark Points**.
